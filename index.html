Research Areas

Quantum Field Theory

Investigating the fundamental interactions between particles and fields in quantum mechanics, exploring the deepest structures of reality.

String Theory

Pursuing a unified theory of quantum gravity through the framework of vibrating strings as fundamental constituents of matter.

Cosmology

Studying the origin, evolution, and ultimate fate of the universe, from the Big Bang to dark energy and beyond.

Condensed Matter

Exploring exotic phases of matter, quantum phase transitions, and emergent phenomena in many-body systems.

High Energy Physics

Investigating particle interactions at the highest energies, searching for new physics beyond the Standard Model.

Mathematical Physics

Developing mathematical frameworks and tools to understand complex physical phenomena and their underlying symmetries.
