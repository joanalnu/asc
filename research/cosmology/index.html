Char for Astroparticle Physics and Cosmology

The group of Prof. Mukhanov on astroparticle physics and cosmology is a part of the TR33 - The Dark Universe. The Transregional Collaborative Research Centre TR33 is a joint network with the University of Heidelberg and the University of Bonn. In addition the group participates in the Excellence Cluster "Origin and Structure of the Universe".

Research

The chair of Prof. Mukhanov is mainly oriented to research including inflationary cosmology and string theory. The group is composed of three profesors and several research associates.
