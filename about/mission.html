Our mission is to foster collaboration among researchers of all levels - residents and visitors alike - across diverse domains of theoretical physics.

Physics @ Munich

Munich hosts a great and broad ecosystem for theoretical and general physics research, hosting:

  • Physics Faculty of the LMU University
  • Physics Faculty of the TUM University
  • Max Planck Institut für Physik
  • Max Planck Institut für Astrophysik
  • Max Planck Institut für Extraterrestrische Physik
  • Max Planck Institut für Quantenoptik
  • Max Planck Institut für Plasmaphysik

ASC's Research Groups

At the ASC, we have the aim to promote and smooth collaboration between these high-profile research institutes and the training institutions. Our job is to bring research closer to the University students and enable them to start doing research from early on. We integrate research in a variety of fields including

  • Computational & Plasma Physics
  • Cosmology
  • Mathematical Physics / String Theory
  • Quantum Matter Theory
  • Statistical and Biological Physics
  • Theoretical Nanophysics
  • Theoretical Particle Physics
  • Theoretical Solid State Physics

Cross-Center Collaboration

In order to fulfill our aims, at the ASC we join efforts with research leading institutions both locally and internationally. Munich has a great network of physics research institutes with which the ASC collaborates and exchanges. We are part of the following projects:

  • Center for NanoScience
  • Origins Cluster for Excellence
  • Nano Initiative Munich
  • SFB 631
  • SFB-Transregio (TRR 33) - The Dark Universe

In addition to that, the ASC has signed Memorandums of Understanding with resonating institutions around the world chasing our goal to promote no-boundaries collaboration. ASC's partners are:
