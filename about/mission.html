Our Mission

Our mission is to foster collaboration among researchers of all levels - residents and visitors alike - across diverse domains of theoretical physics.

Physics @ Munich

Munich hosts a greate ecosystem for theoretical and general physics research, hosting:

  • Physics Faculty of the LMU University
  • Physics Faculty of the TUM University
  • Max Planck Institut für Physik
  • Max Planck Institut für Astrophysik
  • Max Planck Institut für Extraterrestrische Physik
  • Max Planck Institut für Quantenoptik
  • Max Planck Institut für Plasmaphysik

The Arnold Sommerfeld Center for Theoretical Physics has the aim to promote and smooth collaboration between these high-profile research institutes and the training institutions. Our job is to bring research closer to the University students and enable them to start doing research from early on.

The Center integrates partnerships across a variety of fields in theoretical physics, including:

  • Cosmology
  • Particle Physics
  • Mathematical Physics & String Theory
  • Solid-State and Nanophysics
  • Statistical and Biophysics
  • Computational Physics
  • Quantum Optics
  • Quantum Information
