Chair for Astroparticle Physics and Cosmology

The group of Professor Mukhanov on astroparticle physics and cosmology is part of the TR33 - The Dark Universe. The Transregional Collaborative Research Center TR33 is a joint network with the University of Heidelberg and the University of Bonn. In addition, the group participates in the Excelence Cluster "Origin and Structure of the Universe"

The Char ouf Professor Mukhanov works in theoretical astroparticle physics and cosmology. Fields of research include inflationary cosmology and string theory. A detailed description of our research interests is given here. The group is composed of three professors and several research associates.
